Business & Development

Business in Macon County

Macon County supports local businesses and encourages economic growth through resources, permitting services, and community partnerships. Whether you are starting a new business or expanding an existing one, the county provides tools to help you succeed.

Starting a Business

  1. Register your business name
  2. Apply for required permits and licenses
  3. Review zoning and land use regulations
  4. Contact the county for additional guidance

Permits & Licensing

Macon County requires permits for certain types of construction, development, and business operations. Visit the Services page to access application forms and additional information.
